Jaeuk Kim

AI Research Engineer · Computer Vision & Multimodal AI · VLM/RAG Systems · Quantum ML

NextITS

Seoul, South Korea

I am an AI Research Engineer at NextITS building multimodal AI systems — computer vision, generative AI, and VLM/RAG-based decision-support systems — with a parallel research line in quantum machine learning.

My main focus is applying multimodal AI systems to real-world problems: object detection, CCTV monitoring, image restoration, diffusion models, and clinical information retrieval. I ship deployable, edge-oriented AI platforms. Alongside this, I explore hybrid quantum–classical models, building and validating Qiskit-based quantum circuits on real QPUs.

My academic background is in adversarial machine learning, AI security, deep learning robustness, and model vulnerability analysis (M.S., Artificial Intelligence and Information Security, Yonsei University). I previously co-founded Leocompany, where I served as CTO and led an AI-powered product built on generative image models.

Research interests: computer vision & object detection · vision-language models · diffusion models & image restoration · VLM/RAG decision support · CCTV/video monitoring · medical AI · edge AI & model deployment · adversarial ML & AI security · quantum machine learning · hybrid quantum–classical systems.
